# Privacy Policy for SleepyMommy

**Last updated:** March 13, 2026

BabySleep built the **SleepyMommy** app as a freemium app with optional in-app purchases. This SERVICE is provided by BabySleep and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

---

## Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, the app may ask you to provide certain information such as baby names, birth dates, and health-related data (sleep, feeding, growth, vaccination records). All information you provide is stored **locally on your device** and is not transmitted to our servers.

We do **not** collect, store, or share personally identifiable information externally.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

SleepyMommy stores the following data **locally** on your device using a secure local database:

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Baby profile (name, birth date, gender) | Personalization and tracking |
| Sleep sessions (start/end times, duration) | Sleep pattern analysis |
| Feeding records | Feeding schedule tracking |
| Growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference) | Growth chart and analytics |
| Vaccination records | Vaccine schedule tracking |
| Photo and video memories | Personal baby album |
| App preferences and settings | App personalization |

This data **never leaves your device** and is not shared with us or any third party.

---

## Microphone Access

SleepyMommy requests access to your device's **microphone** for the baby monitor feature, which detects baby crying sounds and sends local notifications. Audio is processed in real time on your device and is **never recorded, stored, or transmitted**.

---

## Media / Photo Access

SleepyMommy requests access to your device's **photo library** (`READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`) to allow you to add photos and videos to the Memories section of the app. Selected media is stored locally on your device within the app's private storage.

---

## Notifications

The app uses local notifications (not push notifications via external server) to remind you about:
- Feeding and sleep schedules
- Vaccination due dates
- Baby care tips and milestone alerts

These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external service.

---

## In-App Purchases

SleepyMommy offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features. Purchases are processed securely by **Google Play Billing**. We do not collect or store your payment information. For purchase-related privacy details, please refer to [Google Play's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## Background Services

To enable features like the baby monitor and scheduled reminders, the app runs background services using Android's Foreground Service API. These services operate entirely on your device and do not transmit data externally.

---

## Log Data

In the event of an error in the app, we may collect data called Log Data on your device. This Log Data may include information such as your device's operating system version, the configuration of the app, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

---

## Third-Party Services

SleepyMommy does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services. The only third-party integration is **Google Play Billing** for in-app purchases, which is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## Security

We value your trust. All data collected by the app is stored using Android's secure local database (Room). We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Children's Privacy

SleepyMommy is designed for parents and caregivers. The app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information **from children under the age of 13**. If you believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

- **Access:** View your data directly within the app
- **Correction:** Edit any information directly in the app
- **Deletion:** Delete individual records or uninstall the app to remove all data
- **Export:** (If available in your app version) Export your data from app settings

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
